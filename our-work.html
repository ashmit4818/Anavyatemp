Programs and Interventions

Anavya Foundation creates pathways for inclusive growth across education, skill development, health, environment, livelihood, and animal welfare.

Our Program Areas

Context-specific interventions designed with communities and implemented with measurable outcomes.

Education and Skill Development

Teacher training, digital literacy, and vocational pathways for learners and youth.

Health and Sanitation

Awareness campaigns, preventive healthcare outreach, and hygiene-focused initiatives.

Environment and Sustainability

Tree plantation, sustainability awareness, and local environmental stewardship efforts.

Livelihood and Empowerment

Capacity building and livelihood-centered solutions that strengthen resilience and growth.

Animal Welfare

Street-animal feeding drives, rescue support, and awareness through community care.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Partnerships with NGOs, governments, and organizations to scale sustainable impact.

Our Impact So Far

  • Empowering educators: Trained 40+ teachers, positively influencing 740+ students.
  • Conducted baseline studies on door-to-door workers in North and West Zones.
  • Launched Digital Literacy Project for 160+ students in government schools.
  • Planted 30+ saplings at ITI Savli to promote sustainability.
  • Collaborated with Aditya Birla for POSH training initiatives.
  • Supported street animals through feeding drives and community care.

CSR Partnerships

At Anavya Foundation, collaboration is the cornerstone of sustainable impact. We partner with corporates, institutions, and government bodies to co-create CSR initiatives that align with organizational values while delivering measurable social change.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Credibility and Compliance: Registered under Section 8, 12A and 80G certified, with CSR Registration No. CSR00066840.
  • Tailored Programs: Context-specific interventions aligned with your CSR priorities.
  • Impact Measurement: Rigorous monitoring and evaluation with tangible outcomes.
  • Community Trust: Strong grassroots presence for effective implementation.
  • Innovation and Creativity: Research-driven approaches for long-lasting change.

Areas of Collaboration

  • Education and Skill Development: Teacher training, digital literacy, vocational programs.
  • Health and Sanitation: Awareness campaigns, preventive healthcare, hygiene initiatives.
  • Environment and Sustainability: Tree plantation, waste management, renewable energy projects.
  • Livelihood and Empowerment: Capacity building, entrepreneurship support, financial literacy.
  • Animal Welfare: Feeding drives, rescue support, and community awareness.

Our Corporate Collaborations

We have successfully partnered with organizations such as Aditya Birla Group for POSH training initiatives, demonstrating our ability to deliver impactful and professional CSR programs.

Trust Marker: Anavya Foundation is 12A and 80G certified, with CSR Registration No. CSR00066840.
