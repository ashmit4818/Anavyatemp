About Us

Founded on the belief that collaboration fuels transformation, Anavya Foundation is committed to creating sustainable impact through research, innovation, and community empowerment.

Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Anavya Foundation is committed to creating sustainable and transformative impact through collaboration with NGOs, governments, and organizations. Registered under Sections 12A(a) and 80G, with CSR Registration No. CSR00066840, we bridge gaps between stakeholders to deliver meaningful change.

Vision

To drive inclusive and sustainable development by empowering communities and fostering collaboration.

Mission

  • Research: Conduct research to design practical, long-lasting solutions.
  • Implement: Implement impactful programs that address community needs.
  • Empower: Empower communities with self-sustaining initiatives for resilience and growth.

Programs and Interventions: Our Impact So Far

  • Empowering educators: Trained 40+ teachers, positively influencing 740+ students.
  • Conducted baseline studies on door-to-door workers in North and West Zones.
  • Launched Digital Literacy Project for 160+ students in government schools.
  • Planted 30+ saplings at ITI Savli to promote sustainability.
  • Collaborated with Aditya Birla for POSH training.
  • Supported street animals through feeding drives and community care.
