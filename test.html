About Us

Every meaningful change begins with a belief - that empowered communities can shape a sustainable future. Established in 2023, Anavya Foundation is a Section 8 organization dedicated to advancing social impact through research-led, collaborative, and community-driven approaches. We design and implement evidence-based interventions across key sectors including Education, Skill Development, Health, Hygiene and Sanitation, Environment, Livelihood, and Animal Welfare. Our work focuses on bridging critical gaps between communities, institutions, and stakeholders to deliver measurable and lasting outcomes. Registered under Sections 12A(a) and 80G, Anavya Foundation is committed to transparency, accountability, and creating scalable solutions that contribute to inclusive and sustainable development.
